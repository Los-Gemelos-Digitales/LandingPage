Welcome to TwinGuard Education

At TwinGuard Education, our mission is to offer high-quality services to society through the use of emerging technologies, creating a positive and lasting impact. We aspire to be the leading startup in solving social issues, leveraging digital twin technology to transform the sectors that need it most.

What We Offer

  • We create digital twins of the school environment that allow simulation of various emergency situations, helping to identify risk areas and optimize responses.
  • Our platform offers real-time analysis of the data collected during simulations, facilitating informed decision-making and continuous improvement of security protocols.
  • We provide evacuation routes tailored to the specific characteristics of each school, ensuring that all students and staff know the path to follow in case of an emergency.
  • We generate graphs and reports on simulation performance, allowing institutions to assess their preparedness and make necessary adjustments to enhance safety.
  • We offer training programs for staff and students, ensuring that everyone is prepared and familiar with emergency and evacuation procedures.
